About SkillProof

SkillProof is an AI-based platform that helps students and professionals prove communication, leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving skills through verified digital certificates.

The Problem

Only 42% of graduates are job-ready. Students struggle to prove soft skills beyond marks and resumes, while employers face difficulty finding workplace-ready candidates.

Our Solution

SkillProof uses AI-based simulations to evaluate real-world skills and provides trusted, shareable certificates to improve employability.
